Contact

If for any reason you want to contact me - Sam Hesp - the creator of this website, feel free to send me an email at: “samuel.hesp7 (at) the standard Gmail ending”. I’m open to any comments, questions, criticisms or suggestions (please let me know if you find any broken links or typos). If you happen to live in or near Perth, Western Australia and would like some of my varieties, send me an email and we can discuss further (no deliveries). I usually have quite a few cuttings potted up and rooted for sale but can also sell ordinary cuttings. If there’s a picture of the variety up on the Home page, there’s a good chance I’m ready to sell that variety, but there’s no harm in asking after the others (I may be able to spare a cutting or two). Alternatively, you can usually find me on Gumtree and Facebook Marketplace during the warmer months.
