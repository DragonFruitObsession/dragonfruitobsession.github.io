Forward

I, the creator of this website, am a hobbyist dragon fruit grower based in Perth, Western Australia. I created this website to document my progress in growing dragon fruit by displaying and analysing each of my varieties in an elegant and efficient manner. Some of the varieties I identified through guesswork and may not be accurate, while other unknown varieties I named myself. I hope my analysis of each variety is useful to fellow growers and that this website can expand and become better with time. Click on the pictures below for more information on each variety.
